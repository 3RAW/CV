Actionaid

In 2015 I started to fundraise for a charity called Actionaid. It helps children in less privileged areas of the world like cambodia. I sponsored a child in Myanmar and started raising money by selling lollipops and an old bike which was too small for me. People donated money and plus, a corporate sponsorship gave us £300, so in total we raised £625! You can even check out our site here!

Hello Belmont

When I was 8, I had an idea to make the school magazine as they had stopped making them last year. I interviewed a couple of fellow students about what they thought about the school and even the new head master who had recently joined the school. I put together the information, along with a comic made by my sister, a trick to learn your times tables and lots more! The following year I made the second edition of Hello Belmont. You can read my first issue here and my second issue here!

Mini Medals

When I was on my skiing holiday, I thought that maybe just like I got a badge in skiing, maybe I could get the same thing for rewarding brilliant behaviour. So, when I got back home in London, my family got to work and each designed two medal designs. We put them into a CAD program and my dad turned the design into a medal. We polished the silver finishes so they shined. Finally, my mum, a graphic designer, created the website and the logo. Click here to view it.
